How Does it Work?

Our Hospital-Grade Disinfectant Eliminates 99% of Bacteria & Virus. The product is applied with an electrostatic sprayer. The sprayer atomizes the molecules of the disinfectant creating a 360-degree wrap around disinfectant technology with maximum action.

Safe for Children & Pets

Our hospital-grade disinfectant is an EPA certified product safe to use around children & pets

Carpets and rugs can collect dust, pollen, pet hair, and other allergens over time. These things not only make your home look dirty, they also make it smell unpleasant.
